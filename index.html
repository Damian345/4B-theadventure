You were on a plane trip to hawaii to visit family and friends. Everything was going smoothly. All except for when you see a strange blue mist form around the plane. You are now the only surviver of a plane crash on an island you did not even know you existed. You will now face many trials such as; losing your sanity, fighting monsters, and fending for yourself. Are you up for the challenge? Or will you die in this island. Alone. Stranded.
